"Explore Nepal: A Gateway to the Himalayas"

"Explore Nepal: A Gateway to the Himalayas" is a cultural and tourism website showcasing Nepal's rich traditions, festivals, and iconic destinations like Everest Base Camp, Lumbini, and Pokhara. It offers travel guides, itineraries, and interactive features, making it a perfect resource for discovering Nepal's beauty and heritage..

MAIN ATTRACTION

  • Lumbini
